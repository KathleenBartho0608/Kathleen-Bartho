About Me

I am a passionate management consultant and entrepreneur with over 20 years of experience in project management, process optimization, and digital transformation.

As the founder and CEO of 99active, I'm working to bring my vision of a platform for shared experiences to life. To pursue this vision with the freedom and agility of a startup, I deliberately maintain financial independence and complement my work at 99active with my role as a management consultant.

With broad industry knowledge - from energy supply and finance to the sports industry - I guide companies in establishing sustainable structures and innovative processes.

My focus areas are governance, risk & compliance, controlling, and reporting, through which I safely guide companies through complex transformation processes.

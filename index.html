About Me

I work calmly, focused, and without unnecessary noise. I learned this early during my studies and parental leave, when I carried two major responsibilities at once. Since then, I know how to stay clear-headed, switch topics without losing focus, and remain composed even in demanding situations.

My professional foundation is the Diplom-Kauffrau für Europäische Wirtschaft (FH) – a German business degree comparable to an MBA – combined with more than 20 years of experience as a professional project lead, PMO lead, and IT project lead. This background shapes how I structure organizations, design processes, and guide transformation programs with clarity and authority.

My additional training as an art & creative therapist and movement coach strengthens my ability to lead teams through complexity with grounded focus, human understanding, and the ability to create clarity in dynamic situations. Whether I work with teams, leadership, or complex transformation programs, I bring this rare combination of deep focus, agility, and steady leadership into every project.

I combine large-corporate experience with the clarity and pragmatism of a startup. I work solution-oriented, process-driven, and always with a focus on what truly works for people in their everyday roles.
